---
description: Get a comprehensive overview of your trading portfolio
---

Show a complete portfolio overview including:
- Account status (balance, buying power, equity, PDT status)
- All open positions with P/L
- Market hours status
- 30-day portfolio performance

Use these tools:
1. `mcp__alpaca__get_account_info`
2. `mcp__alpaca__get_all_positions`
3. `mcp__alpaca__get_clock`
4. `mcp__alpaca__get_portfolio_history` (period="1M", timeframe="1D")

Format with clear sections, tables for positions, and timestamp.
